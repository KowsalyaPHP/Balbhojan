THE GOODNESS MOVEMENT
‘Balbhojan’ – the name connotes ‘food for children’. Going beyond the iteral meaning and expanding the scope of the words, we believe in providing food, clothing, shelter, education, health, mental growth and stimulus, caring, a sense of responsibility, the ability and the desire to extend their hands to other in their own ways.
