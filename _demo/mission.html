MISSION
Children are a nation’s greatest asset and most precious treasure. Providing for their physical, mental and spiritual needs is our responsibility.

Balbhojan seeks to leverage a scalable Information Technology Platform, the Power of Networking and Basic Human Goodness to facilitate “easy giving” and “responsible taking” with high level of visibility to the donors at low and very prudent administrative costs. The entire process is “evidence based”.

Be the bridge between those less fortunate, who need sustenance and succor and those who the ability and desire to reach out to their brethren that need their support.

Expand the network of donors and those receiving donations in a phased manner, so that irrespective of caste, religion, community, etc. the needy get timely and apt help.
