Donate To The Balbhojan Fund
 
The Balbhojan Fund is raising money to help distressed children meet their needs. We seek your support to help us in our Mission.

We take very little money (less than 5%) from donors to cover our administrative costs and operating costs, so that we continue to provide Balbhojan services.

There would be no tax exemption provided for the donations made on the website until we get the 80G certification. Application for 80G has already been submitted and it will be approved shortly.
