Privacy and Security

* To fulfil your requests for products, services or information;

* To comply with any applicable law, regulation, legal process or government request;
* To respond to a legitimate claim, or to address our reasonable belief, that you are violating the agreements or policies that govern your use of the Balbhojan’s product or service;
* To protect the services, products or rights of Balbhojan, including but not limited to the security or integrity of the Balbhojan site;

Your choices with respect to Personal Information

Balbhojan recognizes and appreciates the importance of responsible use of information collected on the Site and in connection with the Products and Services. Balbhojan will respect your legal rights regarding access to, and correction and deletion of, your personal information. Balbhojan may communicate information to you regarding products, services, and special offers available from Balbhojan unless you have opted not to receive such communications or such communications are prohibited by law, although in such cases we may find it necessary to continue to communicate with you regarding your use of the Site and the Products and Services. Except in the particular circumstances described in this Privacy Policy, Balbhojan will not provide your name to other companies or organizations without your consent.

There are other instances in which Balbhojan may divulge your personal information, such as when required by law, regulation, or litigation. We may also disclose information about you if we determine that such disclosure should be made for reasons of national security, law enforcement, or other issues of public importance.

Information Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. These include internal reviews of our data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures, including appropriate encryption and physical security measures to guard against unauthorized access to systems where we store personal data. We restrict access to personal information to Balbhojan employees, contractors, and agents who need to know that information in order to process it on our behalf. These individuals are bound by confidentiality obligations and may be subject to discipline, including termination and criminal prosecution if they fail to meet these obligations.

Credit Cards

We do accept credit card payments for Product Registrations, donations and other Services. When you place orders or access your account information, we offer the use of a secure server. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information you put in before it is sent to us. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure correct use of information, we will employ reasonable security practices and procedures and current Internet security methods and technologies in compliance with Information Technology Act, 2000 and rules made there under. If someone uses your credit card to make an unauthorized purchase, check with the institution that issued your card to find out what the exact liability is.

Changes

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent. We will also keep prior versions of this Privacy Policy in an archive for your review.
